Cookies

To improve your experience on our site, we may use ‘cookies’. Cookies are an industry standard and most major web sites use them. A cookie is a small text file that our site may place on your computer as a tool to remember your preferences. 2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you access our website. They help us recognize your device and enhance your experience by, for example, remembering your preferences.
