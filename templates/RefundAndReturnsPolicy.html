TSAR-IT PRIVATE LIMITED RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

At TSAR-IT Private Limited, we are committed to ensuring that our clients are fully satisfied with our software products and services. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to assist you with our return and refund policy.

Return Policy

Our return policy 5 Business days from the date of purchase. If 5 Business days have passed since your purchase, we regret that we cannot offer you a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, the software product must be unused, uninstalled, and in the original condition that you received it. Any digital products must not have been downloaded or activated.

Software that has been customized or developed specifically for your company is not eligible for return unless it is defective or does not match the agreed-upon specifications.

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within a certain number of days.

Please note that refunds do not include any processing fees, which are non-refundable. Refunds for services such as consulting or support will be prorated based on the amount of time used.

If the refund is approved, the customer will receive the full amount paid, minus any applicable fees and costs for services rendered.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Downloaded software products
  • Register fees after the initial 5 Business days
  • Services that have been fully delivered or utilized
  • Custom software development services after final delivery

How to Initiate a Return or Refund

To initiate a return or refund, please contact our customer service team at returns@tsaritservices.com. Provide your order number and a detailed explanation of the issue. We will guide you through the next steps.

Please do not send your purchase back without receiving return instructions from our team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our return and refund policy, please contact us at support@tsaritservices.com.
